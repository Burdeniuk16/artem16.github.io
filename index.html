About our cafe

The cafe is suitable for those who cannot imagine their day without aromatic freshly roasted coffee. The establishment has a showroom where you can taste a large number of different varieties: from classic Arabica to specialty coffee blends. You can also order delivery of coffee beans from Coffee Me. It will be free for orders over UAH 500. You can complement a cup of coffee with colorful nuts, raspberry or pistachio croissants, eclairs and the signature chocolate-walnut cake with caramel. For lovers of the unusual, there are cheesecakes that are baked directly in glass jars. Coffee Me positions itself as pets friendly, so pets are also welcome here.
